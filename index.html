Introduction

Biyolta Tea Factory is a renowned tea manufacturing company located in Kirilipana, Deniyaya, Sri Lanka. With a rich heritage and commitment to quality, Biyolta has established itself as a leading producer of exceptional tea blends.

About Us

Since 1920, we have produced and exported the finest and freshest Ceylon black tea across the globe. Our passion for tea manufacturing has brought us to where we are now, developing the most sensational brew with the perfect proportion of liquor to tingle your taste buds with every sip.
